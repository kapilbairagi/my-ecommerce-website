Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fees for selling on your platform?

We charge a competitive commission fee on each sale, which varies by product category. There are no monthly subscription fees. You only pay when you make a sale.

How do I get paid for my sales?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. We transfer earnings to your bank account on a bi-weekly basis after deducting our commission and any applicable fees.

What support do you provide to sellers?

We offer comprehensive seller support including account management, marketing tools, analytics dashboard, and a dedicated support team to help you succeed.

How long does the approval process take?

Typically, seller applications are reviewed within 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your application is approved or if we need additional information.

Can I sell internationally?

Yes, we support international selling. However, you'll need to comply with the regulations of the countries you wish to sell to and handle international shipping accordingly.
